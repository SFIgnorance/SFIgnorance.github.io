What is Speaking From Ignorance?

We typically start with a brief introduction, and give a short sentence on who we are and what our area of expertise is. The first half of our programme is a discussion around a light hearted question submitted from speakers, friends of the show, or listeners. These questions can literally be anything; previous topics have been: ‘On a scale of 1-10 how well can you picture something in your mind?’, ‘What's something new you've tried recently?’, and ‘What's the most interesting place you've travelled to?’ The second half is where you come in. We want to get to know you and learn more about your area of expertise. Feel free to think about what you would like to tell us about your field and what you think the average joe should know about what you do.
